Chef Antoine's Cosmic Cookies

This is my life's work. A cookie that is crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside, and an absolute delight. I hope you enjoy it as much as I do.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • [THE SECRET INGREDIENT]

Instructions

Mix ingredients. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 9-11 minutes. Enjoy.
